SAN CARLOS DE BARILOCHE

The city of Bariloche is located within the Nahuel Huapi National Park, in the province of Río Negro, forming part of the famous “De los Lagos” region in the extreme north of Patagonia. It is a tourist city of excellence, framed by some of the most important natural beauties in the country. Known as the national capital of adventure sports and the national capital of chocolate, the city is surrounded by ancient forests, snow-capped mountains and crystal clear lakes. With incredible panoramic views, landscapes that are colored according to the season, and prepared to make you experience pure adrenaline. Hiking, trekking, hiking, 4x4, horseback riding, rafting, zip-lining, rappelling are some of the many activities prepared for tourism.
